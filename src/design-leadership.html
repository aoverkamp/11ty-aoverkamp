Pixels with purpose. Teams with direction.

As a Design Director, I lead high-performing design teams by combining creative vision with operational focus. I help organizations deliver exceptional product experiences while growing designers into confident, capable contributors. Here's how I drive impact:

Team Leadership & Coaching

I build teams that grow. Through structured 1:1s, design critiques, and mentorship, I help designers identify growth opportunities, build development plans, and advance in their careers. I advocate for their success, from preparing them for promotions to ensuring they’re equipped for bigger challenges.

Creative Direction & Brand Consistency

I ensure our work aligns with brand identity, product strategy, and user experience best practices. I guide designers through creative decisions, keep work cohesive across platforms, and help raise the quality bar through structured feedback and ongoing dialogue (not micromanagement).

Cross-Functional Collaboration

I work closely with product managers, engineers, researchers, and stakeholders to integrate design into every stage of the development process. My goal is to align design efforts with business priorities, ensuring that our team has the right inputs, support, and clarity to move forward efficiently.

Operational Planning & Execution

I manage sprint planning, ticket prioritization, and resourcing to keep projects on track and teams productive. Whether it’s clearing roadblocks or adjusting timelines, I help the design org run smoothly — especially in environments without a dedicated scrum master.

Executive Communication & Strategy

I partner with senior leadership to align design direction with business goals. From building strategic pitches to presenting creative solutions, I help communicate the value of design clearly and persuasively. I'm a go-to collaborator for rapid problem-solving and early-stage ideation.

Systems Thinking & Process Design

I develop scalable design systems, workflows, and best practices that help teams move faster and deliver more consistently. I lead internal workshops, documentation efforts, and hands-on implementation — all aimed at raising quality and improving efficiency.

Hands-On Design Work

Though I lead, I also contribute. I regularly design, prototype, and explore solutions alongside my team. Staying close to the craft helps me lead by example and keep a pulse on the realities of execution.

Accountability & Resilience

When things don’t go as planned — tight deadlines, shifting goals, or executive pressure — I step in to absorb the stress and stabilize the team. I manage expectations, run retrospectives, and help teams course-correct quickly without sacrificing morale or quality.

Looking to build or scale a design team that blends strategic thinking with great execution? I bring a balanced approach to leadership that fosters team growth, delivers high-quality design, and drives meaningful business outcomes.
